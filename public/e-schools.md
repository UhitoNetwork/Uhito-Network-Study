# E-Schools – Free Learning Platforms

These platforms offer free courses (or financial aid) in IT and cybersecurity.

| Platform | Cost | Topics |
|----------|------|--------|
| [Alison](https://alison.com) | Free | IT, Cybersecurity Diplomas |
| [Coursera](https://coursera.org) | Financial aid | Google IT, Google Cybersecurity, IBM, Microsoft |
| [Cisco Networking Academy](https://www.netacad.com) | Free | Networking, Cybersecurity, Linux, IoT |
| [Open University](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/) | Free | Cyber Security, Privacy, Digital Skills |
| [EF SET](https://www.efset.org) | Free | English certification |
| [Oxford](https://www.oxfordhomestudy.com) | Free enrollment | AI, Business Security (certificate upon completion) |
| [ISC2](https://www.isc2.org) | Free (some) | Certified in Cybersecurity (free course + exam) |
| [Udemy](https://udemy.com) | Free courses | Various – filter by "Free" |
| [IBM SkillsBuild](https://skillsbuild.org) | Free | IT fundamentals, Cybersecurity, AI |
| [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com) | Free | Azure, Security, Compliance |

---

**Tip:** Always apply for financial aid on Coursera – it's 100% free and grants full access.
